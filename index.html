Our Story

JustCater is a fine food service ready to cater to all your occasions and needs! We are based in Co.Waterford and are always ready for your next event. We use the finest of ingredients sourced locally and cook in a style that brings out the most authentic of flavors.

Head Chef Gerard Phelan has over 20 years of expertise behind him aswell as many awards collected during his career, some of which can be found in Waterford Institute of Technology. We cater for all function types such as weddings, birthdays, communions and confirmations.

Our menus are tailored for your dietary needs, we offer alternatives for each individual based on their preferences from gluten friendly and vegan options . Take a look at some of our menus below and feel free to contact us to customize your dream menu!
